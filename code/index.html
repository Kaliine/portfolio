My thoughts about code

Oct 2021

Why I code

During a trip to Japan, back in 2017, I came across the expression Ikigai. Your ikigai is your life purpose or your bliss. It's what brings you joy and inspires you to get out of bed every day. I was curious about Japanese wisdoms and attitudes so I started reading more about this philosphy. Ikigai have four fundamental components – Passion, mission, profession and vocation. When these components overlap each other in a balanced way, is when you will find your ikigai. >>
